# IAS Anchor Mathematicians — Chronological Index

Major formulas and models by mathematicians affiliated with IAS School of Mathematics. Entries ordered by **publication year**.

---

## 1960s–1970s

| Mathematician | Born | Origin | Formula/Model | Year | Venue |
|----------------|------|--------|---------------|------|-------|
| **Enrico Bombieri** | 1940 | Milan, Italy | Large sieve method; Bombieri's mean value theorem (distribution of primes in arithmetic progressions) | 1960s–1974 | *Le grand crible dans la théorie analytique des nombres*, Astérisque 1974 |
| **Robert MacPherson** | 1944 | Lakewood, Ohio, USA | Intersection homology (with Mark Goresky) | 1974 | Inventiones Mathematicae |
| **Phillip Griffiths** | 1938 | Raleigh, USA | Hodge theory; variations of Hodge structure; complex geometry | 1960s–2010s | Multiple; *Hodge Theory, Complex Geometry, and Representation Theory* (2013) |

**Awards:** Bombieri — Fields Medal 1974

## 1980s–1990s

| Mathematician | Born | Origin | Formula/Model | Year | Venue |
|----------------|------|--------|---------------|------|-------|
| **Pierre Deligne** | 1944 | Brussels, Belgium | Weil conjectures proof (see IHES reference) | 1974 | IHES Publications |
